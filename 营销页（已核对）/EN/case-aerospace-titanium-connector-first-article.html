Aerospace Case

First Article Verification for an Aerospace Titanium Connector

high-strength connection / critical holes / batch traceability

IndustriesAerospace
Part Functionhigh-strength connection / critical holes / batch traceability
Manufacturing Capabilitiestitanium machining, heat-treatment coordination, CMM recheck and FAI documentation

Project Scenario

The project set up first article verification around titanium machinability, tool wear, hole accuracy and traceable batch data.

Manufacturing Plan

01Part Function ReviewConfirm assembly position, load conditions, operating environment and validation goals.
02Process and Material MatchingMatch process, material, tolerance, surface and batch requirements before production.
03Inspection and DeliveryPrepare delivery records around critical dimensions, appearance and assembly feedback.

Delivery Result

The first article records clarified key dimensions and machining risks, giving the customer a basis for follow-up low-volume production.
