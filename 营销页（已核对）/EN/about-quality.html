About Us/Quality Assurance

Quality assurance system covering review, testing and data delivery

Identify critical dimensions, tolerances, appearance and material requirements starting from demand confirmation, and form traceable records during the first article, process inspection and delivery stages.

01Requirements confirmationIdentify critical dimensions, tolerances, appearance and quality documentation requirements.
02First article confirmationEnter stable production via FAI or sample confirmation.
03Process testingMake process records by batch, critical dimensions and project requirements.
04Data deliveryOrganize dimensional reports, inspection records and packaging information.

Quality Documentation

Provide verifiable delivery materials around certificates, testing equipment, quality processes and customer acceptance requirements.

  • FAI first report

  • Appearance inspection record

What scenarios does quality control cover?

Different projects have different requirements for inspection depth, data format and acceptance criteria, and the quality process will be adjusted synchronously with project risks.

prototype validationFocus on confirming critical dimensions, assembly benchmarks and feedback on design modifications.
low-volume deliveryPay attention to batch consistency, process sampling and first article confirmation records.
Repurchase projectPrecipitate materials, processes, testing and packaging information to reduce repeated communication.

Need to confirm the scope of quality documentation by project?

Tell us your acceptance criteria, critical dimensions, and deliverable document requirements, and the engineering team will evaluate the inspection path simultaneously.
