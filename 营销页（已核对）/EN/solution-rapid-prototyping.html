Rapid prototyping and iteration

Use a combination of CNC, 3D printing, sheet metal and Surface Finishing to support functional verification, appearance verification and assembly verification, shortening the distance from idea to physical object.

Functional prototypeAppearance sampleAssembly verificationmini-batch iteration

Choose the right prototype path

The prototype page is based on R&D verification, and the delivery time and cost are affected by materials, complexity, quantity, post-processing and testing requirements.

CNC functional prototype

Suitable for parts with real materials, assembly verification and higher accuracy requirements.

3D printing verification

Ideal for complex structures, internal passages, and early proof of concept.

Sheet metal and appearance parts

Ideal for racks, chassis, enclosures and low-volume structural verification.

Data preparation

01
model fileIt is recommended to provide STEP, IGES, STL or 3MF, and indicate the unit and version.
02
Drawings and TolerancesCritical dimensions, tolerances, threads, surfaces and inspection requirements shall be stated on the drawings.
03
Quantity and useDescribe verification goals, quantities, assembly objects, and whether multiple iterations are required.
