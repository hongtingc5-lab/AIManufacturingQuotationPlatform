Solution Center/Instant Quoting

Instant Quoting

From model upload, drawing supplement, engineering review to quotation confirmation, we help customers quickly organize parts requirements into evaluable and communicable manufacturing plans.

Quote Inputs

Supports STEP, STL, IGES, DWG and other files, and can also supplement material, quantity, Surface Finishing, tolerance and quality documentation requirements.

model fileMaterial selectionQuantity stagePost-processingQuality Documentation

Recommended preparation

  • 3D model or 2D drawing
  • Target materials and quantities
  • Surface preparation and inspection requirements
  • Expected delivery date

Evaluation Results

The quotation results will aggregate cost breakdown, risk tips and engineering suggestions to facilitate customers to synchronize R&D, procurement and project decisions.

Manufacturing feasibilityStructural, bore diameter, thin walls, setup and tool path risks.
cost splitMaterials, processing, post-processing, testing and packaging.
Delivery date judgmentSuggestions on the rhythm of samples, low-volumees and stable repurchase.

From Quote to Project Kickoff

The quotation is not a separate price number, but the basis for the R&D, procurement and project leaders to jointly confirm the manufacturing path.

R&DIdentify design risksFeed thin walls, deep holes, tolerances, and Surface Finishing risks early into design iterations.
ProcurementClarify the quotation caliberForm a clear basis for inquiry around quantity, delivery date, materials, testing and packaging scope.
ProjectPrecipitation delivery recordSave quotation, DFM, first article and inspection data to support subsequent repurchase and batch copying.
