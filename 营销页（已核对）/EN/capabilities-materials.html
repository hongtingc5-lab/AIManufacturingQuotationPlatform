Materials

Select materials by strength, weight, corrosion resistance, conductivity, insulation, finishing needs and manufacturing route.

Material Categories

Common materials are organized around lightweight structures, corrosion resistance, conductivity, high-load parts and insulating wear needs, so material, process, finishing and cost choices can be evaluated together early.

Selection FilterNarrow by part requirement

Aluminum Alloys

6061, 7075, 5052, 6082

Low density and stable machinability for housings, brackets, fixtures and lightweight structures.Learn more

Stainless Steel

304, 316, 17-4PH

Good corrosion resistance and durability for equipment parts, shafts, fluid-contact and clean environments.Learn more

Carbon Steel

Q235, 45#, 40Cr

Balanced strength and cost for load structures, bases, shafts and mechanical connectors.Learn more

Copper Alloys

Pure copper, H62, C36000, tin bronze

Strong electrical and thermal conductivity for contacts, heat-transfer parts, bushings and wear pairs.Learn more

Engineering Plastics

POM, PA, PC, PEEK, ABS

Lightweight, insulating, wear-resistant or chemical-resistant options for prototypes and lower-load structures.Learn more

Titanium Alloys

TC4, TA2, Ti-6Al-4V

High specific strength and corrosion resistance for demanding lightweight structures and special environments.Learn more

Composites

Carbon fiber sheet, fiberglass sheet, G10/FR4

Suitable for lightweight, insulating, laminated structures and directional stiffness needs.Learn more
