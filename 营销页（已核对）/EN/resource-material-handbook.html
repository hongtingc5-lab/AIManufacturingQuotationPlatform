Materials Selection Handbook

Quickly screen common metals and engineering plastics based on strength, weight, corrosion resistance, temperature, appearance and cost.

Aluminum AlloysStainless SteelCopper AlloysEngineering Plastics

Quick material judgment

This page is used as a reference for early material selection. Specific grades, performance and compliance requirements are subject to project drawings, supplied materials and test results.

Aluminum Alloys

It has low weight and good processability, and is often used in brackets, shells, heat dissipation and structural prototypes.

Stainless Steel

Consistent strength and corrosion resistance, suitable for connections, equipment components and wear-resistant structures.

Engineering Plastics

Suitable for insulation, lightweight and low-friction scenarios, where temperature, rigidity and dimensional stability need to be paid attention to.

List of material selection questions

01
What loads are the parts subjected to?Stretch, impact, wear, temperature and fatigue can influence material selection.
02
Does the environment have corrosion or insulation requirements?Humidity, chemical media, electrical isolation and outdoor environments need to be explained in advance.
03
Is Surface Finishing required?Anodizing, electroplating, spraying, passivation, etc. will affect appearance, size and cycle.
