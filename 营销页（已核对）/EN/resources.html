PROCESS 01

CNC Machining

High-precision material removal processing through computer-controlled machine tools, suitable for small to large batch production of metal and plastic parts.

Materials

Aluminum alloy, stainless steel, carbon steel, copper alloy, titanium alloy, engineering plastics (POM, PEEK, nylon)

Accuracy

Standard ±0.05mm, Precision ±0.01mm, Ultra-precision ±0.005mm

Surface Roughness

Ra 0.8-3.2μm (standard), Ra 0.4μm (finished), Ra 0.2μm (mirror)

Lead Time

3-7 days (standard), 7-14 days (complex), 14-30 days (precision mold)

Processing Flow

  1. Drawing ReviewConfirm dimensional tolerance, geometric tolerance and surface requirements
  2. Process PlanningDetermine processing sequence, tool path and fixture plan
  3. Programming SimulationCAM programming and processing simulation verification
  4. First Article TrialSmall batch trial production, verify dimensions and surface quality
  5. Mass ProductionFull dimension inspection, SPC process control
View Full CNC Guide →
PROCESS 02

3D Printing

Additive manufacturing technology that forms complex geometric structures through layer-by-layer material accumulation, suitable for rapid prototyping and small batch customization.

Process Types

SLA (Photocuring), SLS (Selective Laser Sintering), FDM (Fused Deposition), SLM (Metal Laser Melting)

Accuracy

SLA ±0.1mm, SLS ±0.2mm, FDM ±0.3mm, SLM ±0.05mm

Layer Thickness

0.025mm (micro-nano), 0.05-0.1mm (precision), 0.2-0.3mm (standard)

Lead Time

1-3 days (rapid prototype), 3-7 days (functional part), 7-14 days (metal part)

Printing Flow

  1. Model PreparationSTL file repair, wall thickness check and support addition
  2. Slicing ProcessingLayer thickness, fill rate and printing direction optimization
  3. Print MonitoringReal-time temperature, interlayer adhesion and deformation control
  4. Post ProcessingSupport removal, surface polishing and heat treatment
  5. Quality InspectionDimensional scanning, density testing and mechanical property verification
View Full 3D Printing Guide →
PROCESS 03

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Laser cutting, CNC bending and stamping forming for efficient production of thin metal parts.

Materials

Cold rolled steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, thickness 0.5-6mm

Accuracy

Cutting ±0.1mm, Bending ±0.2mm, Hole position ±0.05mm

Surface Finishing

Painting, electroplating, anodizing, brushing, passivation

Lead Time

2-5 days (simple), 5-10 days (complex welded)

Processing Flow

  1. Drawing ReviewConfirm unfolded dimensions, bending sequence and tolerance requirements
  2. Laser CuttingHigh-precision cutting and blanking
  3. CNC BendingBending forming in sequence, control springback
  4. Welding AssemblySpot welding or arc welding if required
  5. Surface FinishingGrinding, painting or electroplating
View Full Sheet Metal Guide →
PROCESS 04

Injection Molding

Thermoplastic materials melted and injected into mold cavity under high pressure, suitable for mass production of plastic parts.

Materials

ABS, PP, PC, PA, POM, PEEK, TPE, etc.

Accuracy

General ±0.1mm, Precision ±0.05mm, Ultra-precision ±0.02mm

Mold Life

100K shots (aluminum), 300-500K shots (steel), 1M shots (hardened)

Lead Time

7-14 days (prototype), 20-35 days (soft mold), 35-60 days (hard mold)

Molding Flow

  1. Mold DesignDetermine parting surface, gate position and cooling system
  2. Mold ManufacturingCNC machining, EDM and polishing
  3. Trial Mold AdjustmentOptimize process parameters, verify dimensions and appearance
  4. Mass ProductionStabilize process, control cycle time and quality
View Full Injection Molding Guide →
PROCESS 05

Casting

Molten metal poured into mold cavity to obtain blank, suitable for complex shapes and large metal parts.

Process Types

Sand casting, investment casting, die casting, centrifugal casting, continuous casting

Materials

Cast iron, cast steel, aluminum alloy, copper alloy, zinc alloy, magnesium alloy

Accuracy

CT7-CT9 (sand), CT4-CT6 (investment), CT3-CT5 (die casting)

Lead Time

15-30 days (sand), 20-45 days (investment), 10-20 days (die casting)

Casting Flow

  1. Pattern MakingWood, metal or wax pattern production
  2. Mold/Sand CoreMolding, core making and drying
  3. Melting & PouringMetal melting, refining and casting
  4. Cleaning & InspectionRemove gating system, sand blasting and NDT
View Full Casting Guide →
PROCESS 06

Surface Finishing

Post-processing processes to improve part appearance, corrosion resistance and functional characteristics.

Materials

Aluminum alloy, stainless steel, carbon steel, copper alloy, titanium alloy, engineering plastics

Process Types

Anodizing, electroplating, painting, powder coating, heat treatment, laser marking, polishing, passivation

Coating Thickness

Anodizing 5-25μm, Electroplating 3-50μm, Painting 40-120μm

Lead Time

2-5 days (standard), 5-10 days (complex), 7-14 days (special process)

Finishing Flow

  1. Surface PreparationDegreasing, pickling, grinding to remove oxide layer and impurities
  2. Process ImplementationSurface finishing according to selected process
  3. Quality InspectionCoating thickness, hardness, adhesion and corrosion resistance testing
