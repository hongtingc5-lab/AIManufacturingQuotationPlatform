PAIN POINTS

Common Customer Pain Points

When industrial FDM moves from prototypes into functional parts and low-volume production, problems often come from materials, parameters, validation data and fragmented responsibility.

01Limited material optionsBasic PLA or ABS cannot cover high temperature, wear resistance, flame retardancy, ESD, low temperature and corrosion-resistant applications.
02Unclear performance dataWithout mechanical, thermal and moisture data, teams cannot judge strength, dimensional stability or batch consistency.
03Material-process mismatchHigh-temp nylon, carbon-filled materials and PEEK need tuned parameters to avoid warping, weak layers, clogging and cracks.
04Industry requirements are scatteredElectronics need ESD, automotive needs flame retardancy, medical and low-temperature devices need stable special materials.
05Disconnected delivery chainMaterial purchase, print tuning and finishing are often handled separately, making quality issues hard to trace.
06Imported material pressurePPA, PPS and PEEK can create high unit cost, long procurement cycles and slow R&D iteration.
