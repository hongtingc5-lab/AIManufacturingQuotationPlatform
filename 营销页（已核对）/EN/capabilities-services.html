Service flow from requirement to delivery

The service process starts by confirming drawings and goals, then gives process suggestions, lead-time logic and quality document scope.

Requirement Input

Model / Drawing / SampleSTEP, STP, IGES, DWG and PDF can be reviewed; samples or images can support early discussion.

Engineering Review

DFMReview material, wall thickness, tolerance, fixturing, finishing, inspection and production risk.

Quality Delivery

FAI / CMMFirst-article, dimensional, material and appearance records can be defined by order needs.
01Submit requirementsProvide model, drawings, material, quantity, finish and application context.
02Review routeEngineers match CNC, sheet metal, molding, 3D printing or hybrid routes.
03Manufacture and inspectProduce against the confirmed plan with first-article, process and outgoing checks as needed.
04Pack and deliverComplete surface protection, labels, packaging, logistics and agreed inspection files.
