Anodizing

An oxide film is formed on the surface of aluminum alloy through electrochemical means, taking into account corrosion resistance, wear resistance, insulation and decorative appearance.

5-25μmConventional film thickness
multicolorAppearance options
Aluminum AlloysApplicable materials
48hFastest proofing

Improve the appearance, corrosion resistance and wear resistance of aluminum parts

Anodizing is suitable for aluminum alloy casings, brackets, heat sinks and precision structural parts. Before the process, the clamping position, film thickness range, color requirements and key dimension compensation need to be confirmed to avoid the impact of the film layer on the mating dimensions, threads and conductive areas.
